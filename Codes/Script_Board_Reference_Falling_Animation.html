Script, Boards, and Reference for Falling Animation

The falling animation is about an airplane flying from the sky to landing on the runway in the airport. In the first frame, the plane is on a table, simulating its cruising at 10,000 feet in the sky. When I touch the plane from the table, it represents the pilot engaging the landing gear to fly down. The plane goes through clouds, which I simulated using smoke. Eventually, the plane lands on the ground and moves for some distance before it stops. I shot the motion picture using an iPhone 11 smartphone, with different settings and without smoke. Then I shot the plane on the floor coming to a stop. To create the storyboard, I used sketching with different motions. Finally, I used the Sketchbook app, Adobe AfterEffects, and iMovie to make the video for the animation.
